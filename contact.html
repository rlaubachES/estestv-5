Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive social media management including content production, content strategy, community management, and data analytics. Our services are customized to meet each client's unique needs and goals.

What is your process for new clients?

We begin with a discovery meeting to understand your brand, goals, and challenges. From there, we develop a customized strategy proposal. Once approved, we onboard your brand and begin executing the strategy with regular check-ins and monthly reporting.

How long does it take to see results?

While some improvements can be seen within the first month, sustainable social media growth typically takes 3-6 months. We focus on building authentic engagement and long-term brand presence rather than short-term vanity metrics, so this initial onboarding period will be crucial in devloping the foundation for your social media presence.

Do you work with businesses outside Jacksonville?

Absolutely! While we're based in Jacksonville, we work with clients across Florida. Our digital-first approach allows us to deliver exceptional service regardless of location.

What makes Eclipse Studio different?

We let our work speak for itself—we combine strategic thinking, creative excellence, and data-driven insights to deliver results that eclipse your competition. Our elevated approach means precise attention to detail and customized strategies, not one-size-fits-all packages.
